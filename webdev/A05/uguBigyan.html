About Ugu Bigyan Pottery Garden and Restaurant

Located in Tiaong, Quezon, Ugu Bigyan Pottery and Restaurant is a hidden gem that combines traditional Filipino craftsmanship with a tranquil dining experience. Ugu Bigyan, a celebrated Filipino potter, established this space as a venue to showcase his masterpieces and to offer guests an immersive cultural experience. Known for its handcrafted pottery pieces and serene garden ambiance, Ugu Bigyan's place is also famous for serving authentic Filipino cuisine that complements its rustic environment.

History

Ugu Bigyan, an artist with a passion for pottery, started his journey in the art world in the 1980s. His love for clay evolved into a passion that led to the establishment of his workshop in Tiaong. The place began as a quiet retreat where Ugu could work on his craft, but over the years, it has become a tourist destination for art enthusiasts and those looking for a unique experience in nature. Ugu's pottery pieces are celebrated for their intricate designs and functionality, blending traditional and modern styles.

Pottery Highlights

  • Handcrafted Pottery: Each piece of pottery is handmade by Ugu Bigyan himself and his team. The pottery ranges from everyday items like plates, bowls, and vases to decorative items and bespoke art pieces.
  • Traditional Techniques: Ugu combines traditional Filipino pottery techniques with his own signature styles, creating works that are both aesthetically pleasing and functional.
  • Art and Nature: The pottery is displayed in a beautifully landscaped garden, making it an ideal place to relax and appreciate both nature and craftsmanship.
