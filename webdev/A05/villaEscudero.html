About Villa Escudero

Villa Escudero is a renowned plantation resort located in Tiaong, Quezon, offering a unique blend of Filipino culture, history, and natural beauty. Known for its picturesque coconut plantation, the resort features attractions such as a museum housing a vast collection of historical artifacts, a refreshing waterfall restaurant where guests can dine with their feet in running water, and traditional carabao cart rides.

History

Villa Escudero was established in 1981 by the Escudero family, known for their contributions to the agricultural and tourism sectors in the Philippines. The estate has transformed from a coconut plantation to a popular tourist destination, showcasing the rich cultural heritage of the country. The estate features a beautiful collection of artifacts and memorabilia that narrate the history of the Escudero family and the Philippines.

Attractions

  • Waterfall Restaurant: Dine amidst a natural setting where you can enjoy delicious Filipino cuisine while your feet dangle in the cool waters.
  • Museum: Explore the extensive collection of artifacts, including religious icons, antique furniture, and other historical items that showcase Filipino culture.
  • Carabao Cart Rides: Experience traditional Filipino transportation with a fun and relaxing ride through the plantation.
  • Coconut Plantation Tour: Discover the beauty of the coconut trees and learn about the various products derived from coconuts.

Dining Options

Villa Escudero offers a unique dining experience at its famous waterfall restaurant. Guests can enjoy a buffet featuring traditional Filipino dishes, including adobo, sinigang, and lechon, all while being surrounded by the soothing sound of flowing water.

Activities

  • Enjoy a guided tour of the museum and learn about the history of Villa Escudero.
  • Participate in cultural performances that showcase traditional Filipino music and dance.
  • Take part in various outdoor activities such as swimming, hiking, and biking within the plantation.
  • Relax in nature while enjoying the beautiful scenery of the estate.
