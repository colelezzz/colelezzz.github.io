meet Mike Wazowski

Mike is a small, green, one-eyed monster with big dreams of becoming a top scarer. Despite not being inherently scary, he is determined, hardworking, and resourceful. At Monsters University, Mike strives to prove that anyone can succeed with enough dedication.

meet James P. "Sulley" Sullivan

Sulley is a large, blue, furry monster with purple spots and horns. Coming from a family of legendary scarers, he is naturally gifted and confident but initially lazy and over-reliant on his raw talent. Over time, he learns the value of teamwork and effort.

meet Boo (Mary Gibbs)

Boo is a curious and fearless little human girl who accidentally enters the monster world. She forms a strong bond with Sulley, calling him "Kitty." Despite being nonverbal for most of the film, her presence profoundly impacts Sulley and Mike, teaching them that humans are not as dangerous as monsters believe. Her innocence and playful nature make her one of the most beloved characters in the franchise.

meet Randall Boggs

Randall is Sulley’s rival at Monsters University and Monsters, Inc. He is a sneaky, deceptive monster with the ability to blend into his surroundings, making him a skilled scarer. In Monsters University, he is initially portrayed as a more friendly character but becomes more antagonistic as he becomes obsessed with surpassing Sulley. In Monsters, Inc., Randall is the main antagonist who schemes to sabotage Sulley and Mike to increase his own success at Monsters, Inc.

meet Celia Mae

Celia is Mike Wazowski’s girlfriend in Monsters, Inc. She is a kind-hearted, multi-tentacled monster who works at the scare floor and has a playful, supportive relationship with Mike. In Monsters University, she appears briefly as a fellow student at the university, where she is part of the same scare team as Mike and Sulley.
