PAWS

In the "PAWS" section, you'll find the website of The Philippine Animal Welfare Society, Inc. (PAWS), an organization dedicated to the protection and welfare of animals in the Philippines. PAWS promotes compassion and responsible pet ownership, helping to create a safe and loving environment for all animals. You can support their mission by getting involved, spreading awareness, and following their initiatives to contribute to the welfare of animals in our communities.
