Get to Know Me

Hi, I’m Coleen Isles, IT student at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines Sto. Tomas Branch, where I am passionate about exploring the vast field of technology and its applications. With prior knowledge in C, C++, Java, Python, and currently exploring web technologies like PHP, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I strive to enhance my skills and contribute to innovative projects. My journey in IT has equipped me with problem-solving abilities and a keen interest in emerging technologies. I am eager to collaborate with like-minded individuals and make a positive impact in the tech community.

When I’m not immersed in coding or tech projects, I enjoy spending time with family and friends, finding balance in the simple things. I love playing games, movies, music, and experimenting with new hobbies like cooking or crafts. These moments of relaxation allow me to recharge, bringing fresh ideas and perspectives back into my work. I also enjoy organizing my thoughts through documenting and brainstorming, which helps me stay focused and motivated. While my passion for technology is my main drive, I believe a well-rounded life helps me stay grounded and creative in all that I do.
